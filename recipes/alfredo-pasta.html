Pasta Alfredo Recipe

Classic pasta with some mouth-melting white, alfredo sauce and chicken to top it all off.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of fettuccini pasta
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into chunks
  • 8 ounces of mushrooms, slices
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1-1/2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of grated Prmesan cheese
  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  3. In a large skillet, brown chicken and mushrooms in butter until chicken is cooked through.
  4. Season with garlic salt and pepper.
  5. Add whipping cream and cook until thick,stirring constantly.
  6. Add parmesan cheese when at desired consistency. Serve over noodles.
