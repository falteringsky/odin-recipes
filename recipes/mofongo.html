Mofongo Recipe

Delicious fried plantain that will melt in your mouth. Melted butter or "chimichurri" is suggested for that sweet combination sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of canola oil for frying
  • 3 cloves of garlic, or to taste
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/8 cup of crushed fried pork skins
  • 2 green plantains, peeled and sliced into 1/2-inch rounds
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat canola oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mash the garlic with the olive oil in a mortar and pestle.
  3. Combine garlic mixture with the pork rinds in a large bowl; set aside.
  4. Fry the plantain chunks until golden and crispy, but not brown, about 15 minutes.
  5. Fry the fried plantains into the bowl with the garlic mixture.
  6. Toss to coat.
  7. Mash the coated plantains with the mortar and pestle until smooth.
  8. Season with salt. Roll the plantain mixture into two large balls or several small balls before serving.
