Skirt Steak Recipe

The well-done skirt steak you can't miss. It will drive you crazy

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of balsamic vinegar, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons of grill seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 pound of skirt steak
  • 2 tablespoons of olvie oil, or as needed
  • 1/4 cup of water, or as needed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 (8 ounce) of can mushrooms, drained
  1. Whisk garlic, balsamic vinegar, grill seasoning, oregano, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl.
  2. Add steak and toss to evenly coat.
  3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator, 8 hours to overnight.
  4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat until sizzling; add meat.
  5. Cook uncovered until liquids have drained from the steak, 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Reduce heat to medium; cover.
  7. Cook steak for 15 minutes; uncover and flip.
  8. Add water to the skillet as needed to keep steak moist.
  9. Continue cooking until slightly firm, hot, and lightly pink in the center, about 15 minutes.
  10. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 140 degrees F (60 degrees C).
  11. Transfer meat to serving plate; reserve liquid in the skillet.
